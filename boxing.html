Boxing seems to be very simple, people who have not practiced boxing will also know how to punch when fighting with others, but normal people punch with the strength of their arms, and those who have practiced boxing use the strength of their bodies to push. On the arm, the front force is the force generated by the small muscles of the arm, and the posterior force is the force generated by the large muscle groups such as legs, waist, abdomen, back to pull the arm up. The force is many times larger than the force of the arm. Although the principle is simple, there are strict requirements for the movement of different parts of the human body, it will be difficult for beginners to practice, Only through professional coaches and thousands of corrections and practices can they master the skills of using force. However, learning how to develop strength is only the first step in boxing, fighters also need to practice advanced techniques such as footwork, defense, combined punches, attack and defense transitions. , fake moves, rhythm control. Even punching is a technique, in addition to practicing movements, boxers also need to conduct special exercises such as endurance, physical strength, how to hit. Before playing, you still need to research and build tactics, in general you need to have 2 options, A and B, which can be adjusted at any time depending on the situation on the field, in a word that is easy to understand.
